# Submit a Proof

Share your verified proofs with the community. Submissions are reviewed and published automatically once merged.

## Requirements

Your submission needs four files: `proof.py`, `proof.md`, `proof_audit.md`, and `proof.json`. The proof must be generated by Proof Engine v0.9.0 or later (the JSON summary must include a `generator` block). Running `validate_proof.py` on your proof.py must return PASS.

## How to Submit

**Step 1 — Fork and clone the repo**

    gh repo fork yaniv-golan/proof-engine --clone

**Step 2 — Generate proof.json from your proof**

Run your proof and capture the JSON summary block:

    python proof.py

Copy everything after `=== PROOF SUMMARY (JSON) ===` into a file called `proof.json`.

**Step 3 — Add your files**

Place all four files in a new directory under `site/proofs/`:

    site/proofs/your-claim-slug/
        proof.py
        proof.md
        proof_audit.md
        proof.json

**Step 4 — Add tags (optional)**

Create a `meta.yaml` in your proof directory to override auto-assigned tags:

    tags: [economics, inflation]
    featured: false

If you skip this, tags are auto-assigned from keywords in the claim text.

**Step 5 — Validate locally**

    python proof-engine/skills/proof-engine/scripts/validate_proof.py site/proofs/your-claim-slug/proof.py

**Step 6 — Open a pull request**

CI runs validation automatically. A maintainer reviews and merges. Your proof goes live on the next deploy.
